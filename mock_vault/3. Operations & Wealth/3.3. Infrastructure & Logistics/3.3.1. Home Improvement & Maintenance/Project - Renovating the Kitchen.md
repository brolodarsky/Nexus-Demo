# Project - Renovating the Kitchen

> [!abstract] Overview
> A comprehensive structural and aesthetic renovation of the kitchen to maximize functional efficiency, minimize long-term maintenance costs, and achieve a premium, modern aesthetic. Designs and ideas will be heavily generated and curated using AI (image feeding, image generating, text conversations, etc.).

## 🎯 Primary Objectives
- **Efficiency:** Optimized workflow (the "kitchen triangle" and beyond), energy-efficient appliances, and smart storage solutions.
- **Cost:** High ROI material selection and strategic labor allocation to ensure premium results within a managed budget.
- **Aesthetics:** A clean, timeless, yet state-of-the-art visual language that integrates with the rest of the home.

---

## 🤖 AI-Augmented Design Workflow
This project utilizes a "Model-in-the-Loop" architecture for design and planning:
- **Image Generation:** Using Midjourney and DALL-E 3 to generate mood boards and structural layouts based on specific prompts (e.g., "Minimalist industrial kitchen with white oak and matte black accents").
- **Image Feeding:** Uploading existing kitchen photos and floor plans to VLMs (Vision-Language Models) like Claude 3.5 Sonnet or GPT-4o for critique and optimization suggestions.
- **Iterative Refinement:** Leveraging text conversations to weigh trade-offs between material durability (e.g., Quartz vs. Leathered Granite) and cost-efficiency.
