# 🤖 AI Summary
> [!NOTE]
> This section contains an AI-generated synthesis of the requirements listed below. Refresh this section as new roles are added.

### 🔥 Most Important Skills (Cross-Role Priority)
1. **Python** — Required by the vast majority of AI/ML roles as the single non-negotiable baseline, specifically including async programming and performance profiling (Abridge, FOX).
2. **AI/LLM Fluency & Orchestration** — Hands-on experience with LLMs, prompting strategies, and orchestration frameworks (LangChain, LlamaIndex, or custom pipelines). Emphasized as the key builder skill for modern GenAI roles (Abridge, OpenAI, Deloitte, Perplexity).
3. **Rigorous Model Evaluation & Observability** — Constructing benchmark datasets, designing experiments to probe failure modes/edge cases, automated and human-in-the-loop evaluations, A/B testing, and implementing guardrails/monitoring (Abridge, Perplexity, FOX).
4. **Production Systems & GTM Deployment** — Building, deploying, and optimizing LLM/software workflows in low-latency, high-uptime production environments at scale (Abridge, Meta PE, FOX).
5. **Polyglot & Enterprise Ecosystems** — Enterprise-grade backend development using Java/Spring Framework (PowerMarket) or Go/Rust/C++ (Meta PE, Perplexity), alongside frontend web integrations (JavaScript/HTML/CSS required for PowerMarket and OpenAI).
6. **Systems & Infrastructure Depth** — *nix, networking, version control (Github), Kubernetes, Terraform for infrastructure and deployment stability (Meta PE, PowerMarket).
7. **End-to-End Ownership & Customer-Facing Advisory** — High-ownership builder execution, acting as a technical thought partner for customers, and managing complete feature execution across the SDLC (OpenAI, Abridge, Deloitte).

### Key Themes Synthesized Across All Roles:
- **Shift to Production and Reliability:** Theoretical notebooks are no longer enough. Deployed, performant systems that scale, handle concurrency gracefully under failure, and optimize context windows/latency are key metrics.
- **Evaluation is the New Front-line:** System reliability requires automated/human-in-the-loop evaluations, benchmarking against baselines, and profiling behavior under real-world clinical or product loads (Abridge, Perplexity, FOX).
- **Orchestration Over Prompting:** Demand has shifted from simple prompting to complex, stateful flow engineering (navigating branching LLM chains, agentic tool use, and sophisticated retrieval integrations).
- **Polyglot Adaptability:** Standard Python is crucial, but integration with enterprise structures (Java/Spring, TypeScript, Rust, or cloud MLOps platforms like SageMaker and Vertex AI) is expected.
