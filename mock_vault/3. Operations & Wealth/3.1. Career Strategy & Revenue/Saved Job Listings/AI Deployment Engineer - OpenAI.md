## Role Overview

- **Company:** OpenAI
- **Team:** AI Deployment Engineering (Go To Market)
- **Location:** NYC HQ (Hybrid — 3 days/week in office)
- **Type:** Full-time
- **Compensation:** $198,000 – $280,000 (Zone A) + Equity

The AI Deployment Engineering team ensures the safe and effective deployment of Generative AI applications for developers and enterprises. This role focuses specifically on **strategic startup customers** — guiding them from ideation through production on the OpenAI API platform.

---

## Responsibilities

- Partner with strategic startup customers as their **technical thought partner** to build novel API applications
- Provide proactive guidance to maximize business impact and accelerate application development
- Experiment and prototype alongside customers, demonstrating practical use cases
- Contribute to open-source resources; codify and share best practices
- Synthesize and deliver high-fidelity product & model feedback to Product and Research teams
- Build relationships within the startup ecosystem as a technical community partner

---

## Required Qualifications

- 5+ years as a software engineer, ML engineer, or equivalent (startup environment preferred)
- Experience as a **founder or founding engineer** highly valued
- Proficiency in **Python and JavaScript**
- Strong grasp of AI/LLM best practices
- Experience building and delivering prototypes on the OpenAI API platform
- End-to-end problem ownership mentality
- Ability to manage multiple concurrent projects and prioritize ruthlessly
