## LinkedIn Strategy

### Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** Target format: `[Role You Want] | [Unique Differentiator] | [Key Skill]`
	- Example: *"AI Platform Engineer | LLM Evaluator & Agent Architect | Python · RAG · Zettelkasten"*
- **About Section:** Lead with the pivot narrative — from AI Rater to AI Builder. Reference [[My Skills]] for accuracy.
- **Featured:** Pin Feeder GitHub, Nexus GitHub, and any published writing samples.

### Content Calendar
- **Goal:** 2–3 posts per week to build visibility in the AI space.
- **Content Types:**
	- Technical micro-posts (things learned while building Feeder or Nexus).
	- Commentary on AI industry news (reference [[Synthesis - AI Superagency & The Future of Work]]).
	- Project demo clips or screenshots.

### Connection Targets
- AI/ML engineers and Principal Investigators (PIs) at target companies and academic labs (see [[Job Hunt War Room#2. Industry Targets|Industry Targets]]).
- Recruiters and Lab Managers specializing in AI/Robotics roles in NYC/NJ.
- Open-source maintainers of tools you use (LangChain, CrewAI, etc.).
- Leaders in the "Hidden Market": Engineering managers at GovTech (18F/USDS), HealthTech startups, and legacy non-profits looking to modernize their stacks with AI.

## Outreach Templates

### Cold Email to Recruiter
> Subject: AI Platform Engineer — open to [Company] opportunities
>
> Hi [Name], I came across [Company]'s work on [specific project/product]. I'm currently transitioning from AI evaluation ($50/hr, LLM output assessment) into AI engineering, with hands-on projects in agent orchestration and RAG systems. I'd love to learn about any [Role Type] openings on your team. Happy to share my portfolio. Best, Will

### Warm Intro Request
> Hey [Mutual Contact], I saw that [Target Person] at [Company] is working on [AI topic]. Would you be comfortable making an intro? I'm specifically interested in [specific role/team] and think my experience with [relevant project] would be a good fit. Thanks!

### Post-Meeting Follow-Up
> Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to chat today. I really enjoyed hearing about [topic discussed]. As a follow-up, here's [relevant link/resource]. I'd love to stay in touch — let me know if there's anything I can help with on your end. Best, Will

### Cold Email to Academic PI
> Subject: Inquiry re: [Lab Name] — [Specific Research Topic]
>
> Dear Professor [Name], I've been following your lab's work on [Paper/Project] and was particularly struck by [Specific Insight]. I'm a Dec 2025 CS grad from Fordham with experience in AI evaluation and agentic orchestration (Nexus.0). I'd love to discuss how my background in building stateful RAG systems might contribute to your research on [Topic]. Are you currently open to research assistants or engineering collaborators? Best, Will
