Use this note to run recurring career maintenance tasks. The system only works if you feed it — these cadences keep the career engine warm whether you're actively hunting or coasting.

> [!important] Recommendation
> Bookmark this note. Review it every Sunday evening alongside [[Protocol - Brain Maintenance]].

## Daily (During Active Job Hunt)

- [ ] **Execute [[Protocol - Daily Operational Rhythm]]** (DSA + 2 Job Apps + CRM)
- [ ] **Rehearse one interview scenario**
	- Open [[Interview Prep Hub]]. Pick one STAR story or one system design question and talk through it out loud (2–5 minutes).
	- Rotate topics so nothing goes stale.
- [ ] **Post-interview debrief**
	- After any interview, immediately add what they asked to [[Interview Prep Hub]]. Your future self needs the data while it's fresh.
- [ ] **Update [[Professional CRM]]**
	- Any recruiter call, LinkedIn reply, or networking interaction → add a row. One line. 30 seconds.

## Weekly (Sunday Evening)

- [ ] **5-minute financial check-in**
	- Open [[Income Streams Hub]]. Confirm Full Sprint status is unchanged. No active bridge income to track.
- [ ] **CRM "Next Action" sweep**
	- Open [[Professional CRM]]. Scan the "Next Action" column. Anyone quiet for 7+ days → send a follow-up using the outreach templates in that note.
- [ ] **Application prep**
	- For each role you're applying to this week, pull the relevant elevator pitch from [[Portfolio Hub]] and tailor it.

## Monthly (First Sunday of the Month)

- [ ] **Network audit**
	- Deep review of [[Professional CRM]]. Sort by "Last Contact." Anyone useful but untouched for 30+ days → send a low-pressure check-in. Prune dead contacts.
- [ ] **Portfolio refresh & Deployment Check**
	- Open [[Portfolio Hub]]. Did you ship anything this month? A Nexus feature, a Feeder milestone, a blog post? **Crucial Test: Is it deployed with real users?** Add it while the details are fresh.
- [ ] **Income dashboard update**
	- Fill in the monthly summary table in [[Income Streams Hub]]. Compare to last month. Are trends moving in the right direction?
- [ ] **Skills sync & Platform Update**
	- Learned something new? Add it to [[My Skills]] first, then update [[Resume - Master]] and [[Platform Profiles]] to match. **Mandatory:** Sync changes to Handshake, LinkedIn, Wellfound, and YC to prevent profile drift.

## Quarterly (January, April, July, October)

- [ ] **Salary data refresh**
	- Open [[Compensation Guide]]. Check [Levels.fyi](https://www.levels.fyi/), Glassdoor, and LinkedIn Salary for your target roles. Update the salary bands table. The AI market moves fast — stale data costs you money in negotiation.
- [ ] **Strategy review**
	- Reread [[Job Hunt War Room]]. Are the target roles and industries still accurate? Has the market shifted? Update if needed.
	- Cross-reference with [[Employer Skill Requirements]] — are companies asking for skills you don't have yet? Add learning goals to [[Current Learning]].
- [ ] **Content audit**
	- Are the outreach templates in [[Professional CRM]] still working? Refresh language if response rates are low.
	- Review [[Portfolio Hub]] writing sample ideas — is it time to write one?

---

> [!tip] The Capture → Review → Deploy Cycle
> These notes follow a simple loop:
> 1. **Capture** — dump information in as it happens (contacts, questions, income data).
> 2. **Review** — look at them on cadence (daily prep, weekly CRM, monthly income, quarterly salary).
> 3. **Deploy** — pull from them when you need to perform (interviews, negotiations, outreach).
> 
> The notes that get used most will naturally grow densest. Sparse notes signal that area isn't active yet — and that's fine. They're ready when you are.
