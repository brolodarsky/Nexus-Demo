## Overview

- **Source:** [Meta AI Blog — Introducing TRIBE v2](https://ai.meta.com/blog/tribe-v2-brain-predictive-foundation-model/)
- **Published:** 2026-03-25
- **Filed:** 2026-04-06

> TRIBE v2 is Meta's first AI model of human brain responses to sights, sounds, and language — a digital twin of neural activity that enables zero-shot predictions for new subjects, tasks, and languages.

---

## Key Takeaways

- **TRIBE v2** acts as a digital twin of human neural activity, predicting high-resolution fMRI brain responses to almost any sight or sound.
- Delivers a **70x resolution increase** compared to similar models.
- Trained on **700+ healthy volunteers** exposed to images, podcasts, videos, and text.
- Enables **zero-shot predictions** for new subjects, new languages, and novel tasks — without needing human subjects in every experiment.
- Consistently outperforms standard modeling approaches on benchmarks.
- Built on the [Algonauts 2025 award-winning model](https://arxiv.org/html/2508.10784v1).

---

## Why It Matters

- Allows neuroscientists to **test theories computationally** without requiring human participants.
- Could accelerate breakthroughs in treatment of **neurological disorders** affecting hundreds of millions.
- Bridges AI development and neuroscience — brain insights can **guide AI system design**.
- Released open-source under **CC BY-NC license**: model weights, codebase, interactive demo.
