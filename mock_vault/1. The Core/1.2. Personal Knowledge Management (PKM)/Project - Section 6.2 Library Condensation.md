# Project - Section 6.2 Library Condensation

## 1. Objective
Refactor the technical library (Section 6.2) in the `Table of Contents.md` to reduce cognitive load ("RAM Tax") and align the knowledge architecture with the **AI Engineer** career path. This involves transitioning from a broad "CS Curriculum" model to a "Tactical Ops" model.

## 2. Rationale
- **Cognitive Load:** The current 14 sub-sections create "Identity Rumination" triggers (reminders of what hasn't been learned) and clutter the master TOC.
- **Career Focus:** AI Engineering emphasizes **Orchestration** (LangGraph), **Production RAG**, and **Tooling (MCP)** over foundational research (Math, Robotics, etc.).
- **Response Prevention:** Removing low-frequency items from the daily HUD prevents them from being used as "checking" materials for OCD-driven identity loops.
